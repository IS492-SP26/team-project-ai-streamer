# Consent Script

Thanks for helping with our class project. We are testing a prototype governance tool for AI livestream agents. The session should take about 8–12 minutes.

Participation is voluntary. You can stop at any time. We will not collect your name, contact information, private messages, medical details, or other sensitive personal information. We will assign an anonymous participant ID such as P01. Your responses may be summarized in our course report and presentation.

Please do not share private or sensitive personal details during the session. The prototype is not medical, legal, or crisis advice, and it is not a replacement for human moderators or emergency support.

If you agree to participate, please check the consent box in the survey or tell the facilitator: “I agree.”
